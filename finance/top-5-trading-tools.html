Top 5 Trading Tools

Amith

@amith

  1. 1.

    TradingView

    TradingView is a widely used cloud-based charting and social platform for traders and investors, serving over 100 million users worldwide.

    It offers advanced real-time market data across stocks, forex, crypto, and commodities, along with customizable technical indicators, drawing tools, and Pine Script for building custom strategies.

  2. 2.

    Sensibull

    Sensibull is India’s first and largest options trading platform, built to simplify derivatives trading for retail investors.

    Founded in 2017 and incubated by Zerodha, it provides features such as a strategy builder, virtual trading, advanced option chains, and FII/DII data to help traders analyze and execute structured, risk-aware option strategies.

  3. 3.

    Screener.in

    Screener.in is a widely used web-based stock analysis and screening platform focused on the Indian equity market.

    It enables investors to research and filter companies using financial statements, key ratios, and custom queries, helping users identify stocks that match specific criteria such as valuation, growth, or dividend strength.

  4. 4.

    Tickertape

    Tickertape.in is a popular Indian investment research platform designed for both beginners and experienced investors.

    It offers tools for analyzing stocks, mutual funds, and ETFs, including stock screeners, financial metrics, portfolio management, sentiment analysis, and market news, with integration for brokers such as Zerodha and Upstox.

  5. 5.

    Chartink

    Chartink is a popular Indian stock market analysis platform used by traders and investors to identify trading opportunities through custom stock screeners and technical analysis.

    It allows users to create powerful scans using indicators such as RSI, moving averages, volume, and price action through simple, easy-to-understand conditions.

    With charting tools, alerts, and real-time market scanning, Chartink simplifies technical analysis for NSE and BSE stocks.
